I am Huawei Zheng, a master student in Software Engineering at Zhejiang University, advised by [Prof. Dazhen Deng](https://dengdazhen.github.io/), expected to graduate in 2027.

My research centers on **data agents** and **trustworthy large language models**, with a particular interest in building reliable systems for LLM safety, retrieval reasoning, intelligent question answering, Text-to-SQL, and Schema Linking.
