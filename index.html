Huawei Zheng

Master Student in Software Engineering
Interactive Data Group (ZJUIDG)
School of Software Technology, Zhejiang University

I am Huawei Zheng, a master student in Software Engineering at Zhejiang University, advised by Prof. Dazhen Deng, expected to graduate in 2027.

My research centers on data agents and trustworthy large language models, with a particular interest in building reliable systems for LLM safety, retrieval reasoning, intelligent question answering, Text-to-SQL, and Schema Linking.

Beyond research, I enjoy films, series, books, music, history, technology, and games that offer fresh perspectives or immersive experiences. Seize the day.— Dead Poets Society

You can reach me at zhenghuawei [at] zju.edu.cn, or find more about my work via GitHub and Google Scholar.
